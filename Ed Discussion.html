Course communication done right

On or off campus, face-to-face, hybrid, blended, online – whatever your class type, you want to encourage more interaction within your courses and student-centred learning, whilst minimizing email.

Ed Discussion flexibility and innovative approach solves this with all your course communication needs in one place:

  • Q&A – student questions get seen by everyone and answers reach the whole class.

  • Course information – share resources and announcements.
